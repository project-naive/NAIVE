Note: 
A sound continuous source that has a start and a end  (start with 0, end with 0)
Has a play state length, and modifiers (e.g. f,p, ., etc)

SoundSample
A piece of info use for an instrument to generate the sound.
Typically there are two types (a enum indicating the type)
1. A timber represented as a series of combination of sine, pulse, saw, etc waves
	When represented with double, with 10Hz wave up to 48kHz components, requires 32kb
2. A typical noise wave unique to the instrument (e.g. the "Clang!" sound)
	stored in PCM

Instrument
Assosiated with a sound sample and play state, interface to render notes to a buffer
